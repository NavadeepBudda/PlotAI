Our Mission

In response to significant crop losses caused by inefficient watering and plant diseases, PlotAI is revolutionizing sustainable agriculture. Our program combines water optimization with early disease detection to conserve water, lower costs, and protect crops. This boosts yields and, in turn, combats the growing issue of global hunger.

Efficient Water Use

Our system calculates the exact amount of irrigation needed for every plot, saving millions of gallons while optimizing growth.

Early Disease Detection

Utilizing a TensorFlow-based deep learning model, we detect plant diseases at the earliest stage.

Empowering Farmers

By combing sustainable water practices with early disease management, we help farmers secure their farms.

Our Features

Water Optimization

We calculate a water coefficient per plot using evapotranspiration rates and crop-specific needs. This makes sure that every field gets exactly the water it needs.

Disease Detection

Our TensorFlow-powered model analyzes crop images to identify diseases at early stages, empowering farmers to protect their crops efficiently.
